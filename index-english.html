Sonorum is a platform in music, art and digital culture, that along with the new technologies and a clear vision of progress and lidership, reaches a congruent scene according to what’s happening in the city of Queretaro.

The general objective of this festival is to redefine the cultural identity of the state of Queretaro projecting it as a vanguardist state.
